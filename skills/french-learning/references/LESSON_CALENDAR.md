## Daily Routine Reminder

**Every day:**
1. ✅ Morning: Learn (20 min grammar + 20 min vocab + 20 min listening)
2. ✅ Afternoon: Practice (20 min speak + 20 min write + 20 min read)
3. ✅ Evening: Review mistakes, note progress

**Golden rules:**
- Consistency > Perfection
- Speak before being "ready"
- Errors = Progress
